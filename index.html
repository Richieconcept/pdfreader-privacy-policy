PDFReader Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

PDFReader is provided by Zainith Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how PDFReader, also shown in parts of the app as PDF Reader or All Document Reader, handles files, permissions, advertising, and purchases.

App Purpose

PDFReader helps users read, scan, edit, convert, organize, share, and manage PDF and document files on their device.

Information and Permissions We Use

  • Camera: PDFReader requests camera access so users can scan paper documents and create PDF files. Camera images are used for the document scanning feature.
  • File access: PDFReader may request file access so users can find, open, manage, edit, convert, and save PDF and document files.
  • Notifications: PDFReader may request notification permission if a user enables document-related notifications.
  • App settings and usage counters: PDFReader may store local settings and daily feature usage counts on the device.
  • Purchases: Premium purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. PDFReader uses purchase status to remove ads and unlock unlimited premium tools.
  • Advertising data: Free users may see Google AdMob ads. Google and its partners may process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data according to user consent choices and Google's policies.

Local Document Processing

PDFReader processes document files, selected images, and camera captures locally on the device for reading, scanning, editing, and conversion. We do not upload user documents to our own servers for these document tools.

Sharing

When users choose to share or open a file with another app, Android's native share or open system sends the selected file to the destination app chosen by the user. The destination app controls how that file is handled after sharing.

Third-Party Services

PDFReader uses Google services for advertising, consent management, Play Billing, app updates, and Play review prompts. Data handled by those services is processed according to Google's applicable terms and privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. PDFReader shares data only when needed to provide user-requested app functionality, such as Android sharing, Google AdMob advertising, Google Play Billing purchases, or other Google Play services used by the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Files created or edited by PDFReader remain on the user's device until the user deletes them. Local app settings and usage counters can be cleared by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Purchase and advertising records are handled by Google according to Google's policies.

Security

PDFReader limits document processing to local device workflows where possible. Data handled by Google AdMob and Google Play Billing is processed through Google's SDKs and services.

Children

PDFReader is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, permissions, or legal requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Zainith Technologies through the developer contact email shown on PDFReader's Google Play listing.
